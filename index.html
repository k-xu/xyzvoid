Portable Space

Around the site, you will find many tiles which originated from this location. Each tile has been modified with a line of text, serving as a portable indicator that defines a spatial relationship. As you encounter them, feel free to reposition or move the into new areas that would suit their descriptor, helping to generate new interpretations of the space they occupy.
